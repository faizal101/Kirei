ABOUT THE NOODLES

Facts about my ramen

Food

Ramen is a Japanese dish. It consists of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a meat or fish-based broth, often flavored with soy sauce or miso, and uses toppings such as sliced pork, dried seaweed, menma, and green onions.

Ingredients

Our noodles are made of natural ingredients, and are freshly grown

Health Benefits

The broth is delicious and has a lot of benefits for the body. It is rich in proteins and it contains lot of calcium, which is good for the bones. It also can improves the blood circulation, and also boost the immune system.
